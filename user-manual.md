# 📖 Admin User Manual - Bad Boys HQ

Welcome to the operator's manual for managing **Bad Boys Fit & Brawl**. Follow these instructions to handle daily gym desk operations.

## 🔑 1. Gaining System Access
1. Navigate to the master gateway login page (`admin-login.php`).
2. Input your designated **Admin Username** and **Master Password**.
3. Use the **Show Password** toggle to verify your characters before hitting authorize.

## 👤 2. Activating a New Walk-In Lead
When a pending applicant pays or registers at the front desk:
1. Locate their profile row in the **Member Lead & Assignment Directory**.
2. Type an initial temporary password into the **Set Password** field.
3. Click **Activate**. The system will silently register their account, update your active member metrics counter, and switch their status badge to **Active Account** without a screen refresh.

## 🛠️ 3. Modifying Membership Metrics & Coach Allocation
1. Click the **Manage** button on any member's row. Their details will auto-load into the control panel.
2. Update their **Account Status** or switch their **Membership Tier Allocation** (e.g., Prime - Combat Sports, Premium - All Access).
3. If they requested a trainer, toggle "Allocate Personal Coach" to **Yes** and choose a coach from the active dropdown.
4. Click **Save Profile Metrics**.
